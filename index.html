My Journey

Learning the adobe suite is not something I would have predicted would be required in my CS education degree path and so when I entered this mandatory course I was confused to say the least. I am not an artistic person and the content of this course while not strenuous was still very challenging for me. By the end I very much enjoyed my time working with these powerful bits of software and although I doubt I will be cracking open adobe premier again anytime soon I am proud of what I made and happy to showcase it in this website. Please Enjoy!
